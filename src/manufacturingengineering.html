Overview

Manufacturing Engineering and Technology presents comprehensive, balanced and up-to-date coverage of the science, engineering and technology of manufacturing. It emphasizes the interdisciplinary nature of every manufacturing activity, from complex interactions between materials to design, process, and manufacturing process and operations.
